Key features of the Personal Safety App

  • Shake for Emergency Duress Alarm!

    Activate the emergency Duress Alarm (Panic Alarm) by simply shaking the phone! Optional setting for automatic recording of video that is sent to your Emergency Contacts.

  • Wearable Duress Buttons

    The wearable duress buttons can be carried discreetly in a pocket, worn on the wrist or worn around the neck as a pendant. A discrete push of the button will activate the emergency Duress

  • Alarm!

    The wearable duress buttons are an optional add-on.

  • Biometric control

    Employees can use fingerprint or facial recognition to use the app even more easily.

  • Man Down Alarm

    If Dial 24/7 does not detect any movement during a customisable period (the default is 10 minutes) then Dial 24/7 will ask if you are OK. If you do not respond, Dial 24/7 will raise the Man Down Alarm for you.

  • 5 Second Check In

    Dial 2/7 will generate check-ins during the day for users to confirm they are OK throughout the day. With a focus on efficiency, each check in only takes the user 5 seconds.

  • Low signal mode

    When data is not available the Dial 24/7 app will automatically switch to low signal mode. Alerts will be sent via SMS.

  • Welfare Checks

    Welfare checks allow you to set regular check-ins throughout your day. If you miss a check in Dial 24/7 is looking out for you and will automatically send an alert.

  • Artificial Intelligence Powered Analysis

    By analyzing historical data, Dial24/7's AI can predict potential risky behavior monitoring the employee movement patterns, deviations from routine, and historical data the system dynamically adjusts thresholds based on context.

  • Journey Monitoring

    Use 24/7 to improve user safety on longer drives. Manage driver fatigue, monitor the safety of the user along their journey, and know they arrived safely at their destination.

  • Web Portal

    The easy-to-use Web Portal gives you the tools to manage your users. Use the Dashboard to effectively respond to emergency situations with GPS locations shown on the map.

  • SMS Broadcasts

    Easily send messages by SMS to one, some or all of your users from the Dial 24/7 Web Portal.

  • Login and Activity Reports

    Comprehensive reporting on logins and user activity so you can see who is using Dial 24/7 and how they are using it.

  • 24/7 Monitoring

    Dial 24/7 Monitoring Service gives you peace of mind that every alarm will be responded to using state of the art technology by a trained Australian operator no matter what.

    Dial 24/7 gives you the flexibility to have your Emergency Notifications sent to the Dial 24/7 Monitoring Service or directly to your Emergency Contacts.

  • Ongoing Support

    Even though Dial 24/7 is simple to use, there are going to be questions from time to time. All support queries are responded to in less than one business day by our support team.

  • Monitor and respond to alerts

    Depending on the size of your team and resources, you can choose to monitor activity in the Hub in-house or outsource to one of our professional 24/7 monitoring and response partners. You can also combine both options to structure a bespoke service that meets your needs.

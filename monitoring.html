Responsive Emergency Control Center

Our Emergency Response Centre (ERC) is the heartbeat of swift actions. A dedicated team stands vigilant 24/7, ensuring the utmost safety for your workforce. These seasoned professionals meticulously triage each emergency, orchestrating responses that can be the difference between crisis and control.

Localized Expertise: A Swift Lifeline

When crisis emerges, proximity to aid is paramount. Our Emergency Response Centre localized expertise ensures the right help arrives promptly. Whether it's a local emergency service or a specialized unit, the Emergency Response Centre dispatches the exact support needed.

In-house

Monitoring your employees in house via the Dial 24/7 Hub is easy, low cost and eliminates the need for paper trails and routine check-in calls. The flexibility of the Hub allows you to decide how you manage your employees and alert responses.

External

We also work alongside professional monitoring partners to offer round the clock monitoring and response services for lone worker emergencies. Available 24/7, external monitoring is a great solution for those with minimal in-house resources, larger organisations or those who operate out of hours such as through the night.

Dial 24/7 Artificial Intelligence(AI) Powered
Secured Cloud Based Real Time Monitoring Hub

Real-time monitoring is the bedrock of effective employee safety management. Dial 24/7 AI-powered real-time monitoring feature leverages AI's prowess to elevate safety oversight to unparalleled levels. Behind the scenes, Dial24/7's AI-powered features are powered by cloud computing. The scalability and computational capabilities of the cloud enable Dial 24/7 to process vast amounts of data in real time, ensuring uninterrupted and responsive tracking and monitoring across large workforces and diverse environments.

Adaptive Alert Thresholds:

AI-powered real-time monitoring doesn't rely on static alert thresholds. Instead, the system dynamically adjusts thresholds based on context. For instance, an employee's movement in a construction site may trigger alerts at a different threshold than in an office environment. This adaptability optimizes alert accuracy and reduces false positives.

Predictive Incident Detection:

Dial 24/7’s AI engine monitors employee movement patterns, deviations from routine, and historical data. When an employee's behavior deviates significantly from the norm or enters a potentially hazardous area, the AI triggers alerts for immediate attention. This predictive incident detection minimizes response times and ensures proactive safety measures.

Contextual Insights for Responders:

Dial 24/7's AI system enhances alerts with contextual insights. When an alert is triggered, responders receive not only the location but also relevant contextual information. This includes the employee's trajectory, surrounding conditions, and potential risks. Such comprehensive insights empower responders to make informed decisions swiftly.

Predictive Behavior Modeling:

By analyzing historical data, Dial 24/7's AI can predict potential risky behavior. For instance, if an employee frequently rushes through hazardous areas, the system can predict a similar pattern in the future. This foresight enables organizations to implement preemptive safety measures.

Wearable Technology

The Dial 24/7 V.BTTN Lone Worker Device exemplifies our commitment to ensuring employee well-being during critical moments. Let's explore the comprehensive array of features that the V.BTTN offers, showcasing its efficiency, advantages, and versatile applications across a spectrum of work environments.

Dial 24/7 V.BTTN Lone Worker Device Key Features

The V.BTTN is more than just a device—it's a lifeline that employees can instantly access when emergencies strike. We recognize that emergencies demand swift action, and having a panic alarm readily available can make a world of difference.

  • Discreet Panic Alarm

    Crafted with discretion in mind, the V.BTTN empowers employees to trigger a panic alert discreetly. Whether confronted with a challenging situation or an individual that raises concern, the V.BTTN allows employees to send a distress signal out of view, ensuring their safety is a top priority.

  • Man Down Alarm

    Accidents can occur unexpectedly, especially in high-risk environments. The V.BTTN's automatic Man Down feature triggers an alert upon detecting a fall or impact, ensuring that prompt assistance reaches the employee in need.

  • Dust and Weather-Proof Durability

    Work settings vary, and the V.BTTN is designed to withstand diverse conditions. Its robust construction ensures suitability for use in any work environment, regardless of whether it's indoors, outdoors, or under challenging weather.

Effortless Check-In and Real-Time Monitoring

The V.BTTN seamlessly integrates with the Dial 24/7 app, delivering an array of benefits that enhance employee safety and peace of mind.

  • Quick Check-In

    A simple press of the V.BTTN button allows users to swiftly check into an ongoing session. This streamlined action ensures that employees can maintain a connection with the system without interrupting their workflow.

  • Convenient Access

    Engineered for versatility, the V.BTTN can be worn in various ways—on a wristband, pendant, or clip. Its compact and unobtrusive design guarantees easy access, enabling employees to activate safety measures promptly.

  • Pairing Flexibility

    Pairing the V.BTTN with the Dial 24/7 app offers tailored options for check-in and panic functions. Users can choose between single-click and press-and-hold options, ensuring a seamless user experience that aligns with their preferences.

Enhancing Safety in Diverse Work Scenarios

The V.BTTN is a versatile solution that caters to an array of work environments and scenarios, ensuring employee safety across the spectrum.

  • Working Without a Phone

    In situations where using a phone might be inconvenient—such as while driving, working at height, or operating machinery—the V.BTTN shines.
